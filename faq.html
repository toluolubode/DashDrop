About DashDrop

  1. What is DashDrop?

    Dashdrop is a delivery management solution that connects you to directly to delivery services with the cheapest prices.

    DashDrop is built for easily scheduling delivery orders, managing multiple deliveries and connecting powerful integration tools. It's also known for multi-delivery capacity and excellent customer support. To see why others have chosen DashDrop, check out what other business owners have to say.

  2. Why chose DashDrop?

    The rest of your business runs at peak efficiency. Delivery should not be your bottle neck. DashDrop gives you a birds-eye view as well as indepth analytics on the metrics that matter. See how DashDrop changed Amir's business.

  3. How can I use DashDrop to power my businesses deliveries?

    DashDrop is your key to convenient and cost-effective shipping for small and medium-sized businesses that sell online and in stores.

    DashDrop connects you the best courier companies and allows you instantly schedule a delivery with any one of them. When you're done with that sit back and know the finances of it all is handled within app.

    Simply download the DashDrop on your mobile phone or use our nifty web version and Voila! If you want a more personalised touch. Get in touch with us here and a DashDrop account executive will be in touch with you.

  4. How can I get in contact with DashDrop?

    Getting in touch with us is easy. Send us an email getdashing@dashdrop.com or give us a call at 647-895-6501. A DashDrop representative is excited to talk to you.

    You can also get in touch with us on facebook, twitter, snapchat and instagram.

  5. How much does it cost to use DashDrop?

    We charge a monthly recurring fee of $10/month. Deliveries cost $7 for the first kilometer and $0.25 for every kilometer after that. The extra costs are for really large packages. You pay once and use across all platforms (Mobile, Web and Shopify plugins).

    We promise they are no hidden fees or surprise charges.
